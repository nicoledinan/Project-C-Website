About Us

An in-depth look at what Project C is all about and how you can get involved to help make a difference in your community.

Mission Statement

Project C is an organization founded on three basic principles: collaboration, creativity and community. Combining the skills of diversified members, we strive to produce quality multimedia content that promotes, highlights, and features local nonprofits, community members and fellow student organizations in order to create a more connected campus.

Values

Collaboration: Working together with other members to create the best content possible and build strong relationships.

Creativity: We strive to create innovative portfolio pieces and gain creative work experience in our industries.

Community: We work with nonprofits, other student organizations, and the Athens community. Our goal is to create a more connected campus and community through the work that we do.
